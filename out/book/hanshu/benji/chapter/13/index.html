五年冬十月，汉王追项羽至阳夏南，止军，与齐王信、魏相国越期会击楚。 高祖五年(前202)冬十月，汉王追逐项羽到阳夏南部，停止前进，与齐王韩信、魏相国彭越约期会师攻击楚兵。 In the fifth year, in the winter, the tenth month, the King of Han pursued Hsiang Yü to [a place] south of Yang-chia. [There] he stopped and encamped. He had arranged for a meeting with the King of Ch'i, [Han] Hsin, and with the Chancellor of State in Weih, [P'eng] Yüeh, to attack Ch'u.
至固陵，不会。 汉王兵至固陵，韩、彭等均未按期会师。 [but even] when he reached Ku-ling, they did not meet him.
楚击汉军，大破之，汉王复入壁，深堑而守。 项羽领兵攻击汉王的大军，大败汉兵。汉王收兵回原壁垒，掘深沟以自守。 [The army of] Ch'u attacked the army of Hans and severely routed it. [So] the King of Han again went into entrenchments, digging deep his moat, and held himself on the defensive.
谓张良曰：“诸侯不从，奈何？” 汉王忧虑地对张良说：“诸侯不愿顺从我们，怎么办？” He said to Chang Liang, "The nobles do not follow [me], what can I do?"
良对曰：“楚兵且破，未有分地，其不至固宜。 张良答道：“楚兵即使被打败，韩信与彭越也不会得到增地的利益，他们不来会师是情理中事。 [Chang] Liang replied, "Altho the troops of Ch'u are almost routed, you have not yet distributed to [your followers] any territory; it is really quite natural that they did not come.
君王能与共天下，可立致也。 要是大王能和他们共同分享胜利成果，他们就会马上前来会师。 If your Majesty is able to share the world with them, you can get them to come immediately.
齐王信之立，非君王意，信亦不自坚。 韩信立为齐王，并非出于大王本意，韩信对于大王还信心不足。 The establishment of [Han] Hsin as King of Ch'i was not your Majesty's design; [Han] Hsin is moreover not yet sure of his position.
彭越本定梁地，始，君王以魏豹故，拜越为相国。 彭越本来平定了梁地，后来大王因为魏王豹来归降的原故，才封彭越为魏相国。 P'eng Yüeh originally subjugated the region of Liang; at first your Majesty installed [P'eng] Yüeh as Chancellor of State on account of Wei Pao.
今豹死，越亦望王，而君王不早定。 今魏王豹已死，彭越也想自己称王，而大王迟迟不予定夺。 now [Wei] Pao is dead and [P'eng] Yüeh hopes to be king, but your Majesty did not decide [to appoint him] in good time.
今能取睢阳以北至谷城皆以王彭越，从陈以东傅海与齐王信，信家在楚，其意欲复得故邑。 要是现在能划睢阳以北至谷城的地区给彭越并封他为王，划陈以东至于渤海的地区给齐王韩信，韩信家在楚地，他本意想收复自己的乡土。 If now you will take [the region] north of Sui-yang to Ku-ch'eng and make P'eng Yüeh king of it all, and give the King of Ch'i, [Han] Hsin, [the region] from the east of Ch'en to the ocean—[Han] Hsin's home is in Ch'u; he wants to obtain again his home town.
能出捐此地以许两人，使各自为战，则楚易散也”。 大王舍弃此二地以许韩、彭二人，让他们各自为战，那楚项就容易打败了。” if you can give up these territories and promise them to these two men, making each one fight for his own [interests], then Ch'u will easily be defeated."
于是汉王发使使韩信、彭越。 于是汉王派使者将上述意向通知韩信、彭越。 Thereupon the King of Han dispatched a messenger to cause Han Hsin and P'eng Yüeh to come.
至，皆引兵来。 通知一到，韩彭都引兵前来会师了。 Both came, leading their troops.

汉亦遣人诱楚大司马周殷。 汉王又派人诱降楚大司马周殷。 Hans also sent someone to tempt the Commander-in-chief of Ch'u, Chou Yin.
殷畔楚，以舒屠六，举九江兵迎黥布，并行屠城父，随刘贾皆会。 周殷叛楚，以舒县之兵猛攻六县，率领九江之兵配合英布，合兵进击城父县，英布与刘贾都与汉王会师。 [Chou] Yin rebelled against Ch'u; with [forces from] Shu, he [captured and] massacred [the inhabitants of] Liu, raised the troops of [the kingdom of] Chiu-chiang, and welcomed [back] Ch'ing Pu; they marched together and massacred [the inhabitants of] Ch'eng-fu. In the train of Liu Chia they all joined forces.

羽夜闻汉军四面皆楚歌，知尽得楚地。 项羽夜间听到四面都是楚歌，知道汉兵已经完全控制了楚兵周围阵地。 In the night [Hsiang] Yü heard the army of Hans on all sides singing the songs of Ch'u, and thought that [the King of Han] had gained all the territory of Ch'u.
羽与数百骑走，是以兵大败。 就率数百骑弃营突围，楚军因无主而大败。 [so Hsiang] Yü fled with several hundred horsemen. Because of this fact, his army was severely defeated.

楚地悉定，独鲁不下。 楚地完全平定，唯独鲁地不降。 [Thereafter] the territory of Ch'u was all subjugated, only [the state of] Lu would not submit.
汉王引天下兵欲屠之，为其守节礼义之国，乃持羽头示其父兄，鲁乃降。 汉王率领大军想要屠城，但又被该地将士为项羽守节持义之举所感动，就停止进攻，将项羽首级悬示以告知鲁地的父兄，鲁地乃降。 The King of Han led the troops of the empire [against it], intending to massacre its [inhabitants]. Because it was a state which had guarded itself faithfully and was [known for its] proper conduct and sense of human relationships, [the King of Han had] the head of [Hsiang] Yü held up and shown to the elders [of Lu]; then Lu surrendered.
初，怀王封羽为鲁公，及死，鲁又为之坚守，故以鲁公葬羽于谷城。 原先，怀王封项羽为鲁公，项羽死后，鲁地又为之坚守，因此就以鲁公的仪式葬项羽于济北谷城。 Because King Huai had originally appointed [Hsiang] Yü as the Duke of Lu, and because when he died Lu was still firmly defended for him, [the King of Han had Hsiang] Yü therefore buried at Ku-ch'eng [with the title of] a Duke of Lu.
汉王为发丧，哭临而去。 汉王为项羽发丧，并到灵前哭泣哀悼。 The King of Han proclaimed a mourning ceremony for him. He wept and lamented, then left.
封项伯等四人为列侯，赐姓刘氏。 汉王封项伯等四人为列侯，赐刘姓。 He appointed Hsiang Po and others, [altogether] four [of Hsiang Yü's kindred], as marquises, granting them the [imperial] surname, Liu.
诸民略在楚者皆归之。 外地百姓被掠到楚地的都遣返原籍。 All the people who had been captured [and kept] in Ch'u were [allowed] to return [home].
汉王还至定陶，驰入齐王信壁，夺其军。 汉王回师到定陶，以迅雷不及掩耳之势进入韩信军营，削夺了韩信的兵权。 The King of Han returned to Ting-t'ao, rode into the entrenchments of the King of Ch'i, [Han] Hsin, and took away his army.
初项羽所立临江王共敖前死，子尉嗣立为王，不降。 原先曾是项羽所立的临江王共敖已死，其子尉继位为王，不肯投降。 The King of Lin-chiang, Kung Ao, whom Hsiang Yü had originally set up, had previously died; his son [Kung] Wei had been set up [by Hsiang Yü] in succession as king, [so] he did not surrender.
遣卢绾、刘贾击虏尉。 汉王派卢绾、刘贾征临江王尉。 [The King of Han] sent Lu Wan and Liu Chia to attack him; they captured [Kung] Wei.
春正月，追尊兄伯号曰武哀侯。 汉高祖六年春正月，汉王追尊其兄刘伯为武哀侯。 In the spring, the first month, [the King of Han] posthumously honored his older brother [Liu] Po, entitling him Marquis Wu-ai.
下令曰：“楚地已定，义帝亡后，欲存恤楚众，以定其主。 下令说：“楚地已经平定，义帝无有后代，想安抚楚地民众，就另定楚地之主。 An order was given, saying, "The region of Ch'u has already been subjugated, [but] the Emperor Yi had no heirs; We wish to be solicitous for the people of Ch'u and fix upon a king for them.
齐王信习楚风俗，更立为楚王，王淮北，都下邳。 齐王韩信了解楚地风土人情，现改立为楚王。统辖淮北地区，都于下邳。 The King of Ch'i, [Han] Hsin, is well versed in the customs of Ch'u—let [his kingdom] be changed and [let] him be established as the King of Ch'u, ruling over [the region] north of the Huai [River], with his capital at Hsia-p'ei.
魏相国建城侯彭越勤劳魏民，卑下士卒，常以少击众，数破楚军，其以魏故地王之，号曰梁王，都定陶。” 魏相国建城侯彭越关心魏民，爱护士卒，经常以少击众，几次打败楚军，就将魏故地赐为他的封地，号为梁王，都于定陶。” The Chancellor of State at Weih, the Chien-ch'eng Marquis, P'eng Yüeh, has toiled diligently for the people of Weih; he has humbled and abased himself to his soldiers and officers. Often with a few [followers] he has attacked a more numerous [force]; several times he has routed the army of Ch'u. Let him be made king over the former territory of Weih, with the title, `The King of Liang.' His capital shall be at Ting-t'ao."
又曰：“兵不得休八年，万民与苦甚，今天下事毕，其赦天下殊死以下。” 在令中又说：“战争已连续八年，给人民造成严重的灾难，今日天下已经统一安定。除已决死刑外的囚犯都一律赦免。” [The order] also said, "The troops have not had rest for eight years. All the people have suffered severely. Now [my efforts in settling the control] of the world have been brought to completion. Let an amnesty [be proclaimed] throughout the world [for all crimes] below [those deserving] capital punishment."

先时，秦为亡道，天下诛之。 以往秦朝无道，神人共行诛讨。 In times past the Ch'in [dynasty] acted contrary to principle and the world punished it.
大王先得秦王，定关中，于天下功最多。 大王首先俘虏秦王子婴，平定关中，功劳盖于天下。 You, great King, were the first to capture the King of Ch'in and subjugate Kuan-chung—your achievements have been the greatest in the world.
存亡定危，救败继绝，以安万民，功盛德厚。 存亡定危，救败继绝，以安定万民，功高德厚。 You have preserved the perishing and given repose to those in danger; you have rescued those who were ruined and have continued broken [lines of descent] in order to tranquillize all the people. Your achievements are abundant and your virtue is great.
又加惠于诸侯王有功者，使得立社稷。 同时又施加恩惠于各个有功的侯王，对他们裂土分封。 You have moreover granted favors to the vassal kings who have merit, enabling them to succeed in setting up their gods of the soil and grains.
地分已定，而位号比拟，亡上下之分，大王功德之著，于后世不宣。 现在各侯王封地及名分已定，而和大王同称王号，没有尊卑之别，如此则大王显著的高功盛德，就不能宣明于后世。 The division of the land has already been settled, but positions and titles are [still] confounded with one another, without the [proper] division of the superior [from] the inferior, so that the manifestation of your, the great King's, merits and virtue is not proclaimed to later generations.
昧死再拜上皇帝尊号。” 因此臣等冒死再拜请上皇帝尊号。” Risking death and making repeated obeisances, we offer to our superior the honorable title of Emperor."
汉王曰：“寡人闻帝者贤者有也，虚言亡实之名，非所取也。 汉王辞谢道：“寡人常闻帝号归于圣贤所有，名不副实的名号，是不应取得的。 The King of Hans replied, "I, a person of little virtue, have heard that [the title of] emperor should be possessed by a man eminent in talent and virtue. An empty name without [possessing] its reality should not be adopted.
今诸侯王皆推高寡人，将何以处之哉？” 今日诸位侯王都推举寡人称帝，寡人怎能领受得起？” Now you, vassal kings, have all highly exalted me, a person of little virtue. How could I therefore occupy [such a position]?"
诸侯王皆曰：“大王起于细微，灭乱秦，威动海内。 诸位侯王都禀道：“大王崛起于民间，消灭乱秦，威震海内。 The vassal kings all said, "You, great King, arose from small [beginnings]; you destroyed the seditious [dynasty of] Ch'in; your majesty stirs everything within the seas.
又以辟陋之地，自汉中行威德，诛不义，立有功，平定海内，功臣皆受地食邑，非私之地。 后来又以巴蜀偏僻之区为基地，从汉中推行威德，诛讨不义，封立有功，平定海内，功臣都得到封地与食邑，一律论功行赏。 Moreover, starting from a secluded and mean region, from Han-chung, you acted out your majesty and virtue, executing the unrighteous, setting up the meritorious, tranquillizing and establishing the empire. Meritorious officials all received territory and the income of towns; you did not appropriate them for yourself.
大王德施四海，诸侯王不足以道之，居帝位甚实宜，愿大王以幸天下。” 大王的恩德遍于四海，其他侯王都不能与之媲美，尊为皇帝是名实相副，愿大王能满足天下臣民的愿望。” Your virtue, great King, has been bestowed [even to the borders of] the four seas. We, vassal kings, [find our speech] inadequate to express it. For you to take the position of Emperor would be most appropriate. We hope that you, great King, will favor the world [by doing so]."
汉王曰：“诸侯王幸以为便于天下之民，则可矣。” 汉王说：“诸位侯王如果说寡人称帝有利天下人民的话，那寡人就认可了。” The King of Han replied, "Since the vassal kings would be favored [by it] and since they consider it to be an advantage to [all] the people in the world, it may be done."

In the second month, on [the day] chia-wu, they presented to their superior the honorable title [of Emperor].
汉王即皇帝位于汜水之阳。 汉王即皇帝位于汜水以北的济阴。 and the King of Han ascended the imperial throne upon the northern bank of the river Szu.

代地吾所急，故封豨为列侯，以相国守代，今乃与王黄等劫掠代地！ 代地是我很重视的地方，所以封他为列侯，以相国身份镇守代地。不意今日竟与王黄等作乱于此地！ I have been anxious about the region of Tai, hence I appointed [Ch'en] Hsi to be a marquis, and, as Chancellor of State, to guard Tai; but now with Wang Huang and others he has seized and ravaged the region of Tai.

上曰：“是力不足，亡罪。” 皇上说：“这是他们兵力不足，无罪。” The Emperor [then] said, "Their strength was inadequate; they have committed no crime."

上嫚骂曰：“竖子能为将乎！” 皇上谩骂道：“小子们能当将领吗？” The Emperor treated them with contempt and scolded them, saying, "You striplings, have you the stuff to be generals?"

上曰：“非汝所知。 皇上说：“这不是你们所知道的。 The Emperor replied, "This is not anything that you understand.

皆曰：“善。” 大家都说：“对。” They all replied, "You are right."

上曰：“吾知与之矣。” 皇上说：“我知道采取什么办法来对付他们了。” The Emperor said, "Then I know how to deal with them."

城降，卒骂者斩之。 攻克后，对于骂皇上的叛兵予以斩首示众。 and, when the city surrendered, those soldiers who had cursed him were beheaded.
诸县坚守不降反寇者，复租赋三岁。 各县坚守不降叛军的，免除三年的租赋。 Those prefectures which had been firmly defended and did not surrender to the rebellious robbers, were exempted from the land tax and capitation taxes for three years.

将军柴武斩韩王信于参合。 将军柴武斩韩王信于参合。 General Ch'ai Wu beheaded the King of Han, [Han] Hsin, at Ts'an-ho.

Consequently, [the state of] Chao has [to control this territory] from south of the mountains. It is far away [from its administrative headquarters], frequently pillaged [by] the Hu, and has difficulty in being a state.

王、相国、通侯、吏二千石择可立为代王者。” 在王、相国、通侯、吏二千石中选择可以立为代王的人。” You, kings, chancellors of states, marquises, and officials who [have the rank of] two thousand piculs, should select someone who can be made King of Tai."

We beg that he be made King of Tai, with his capital at Chin-yang."
大赦天下。 随后下诏大赦天下。 A general amnesty for all the world [was proclaimed].
二月，诏曰：“欲省赋甚。 二月，皇上下诏说：“朕深欲减省赋敛。 In the second month, an imperial edict said, "[We] wish very much to lessen the poll-taxes.
今献未有程，吏或多赋以为献，而诸侯王尤多，民疾之。 今上交国库没有统一的章程，地方官常多取赋敛以上交，而各地侯王则赋敛更多，百姓甚为困苦。 [but] now the offerings [made to Us] have no regulations, [so that] the officials sometimes make the poll-tax heavy in order to use it for offerings [to Us], and for the vassal kings [this tax] is even heavier, [with the result that] the people suffer from it.
令诸侯王、通侯常以十月朝献，即郡各以其口数率，人岁六十三钱，以给献费。” 现在通知各侯王、通侯每逢十月为上交赋敛期，至于各地计算的方式是以人口数为准，每人每年上缴六十三钱，以交国库。” Henceforth the vassal kings and marquises shall regularly pay court and make offerings [to Us, the Emperor], in the tenth month, and each commandery shall [make an offering] in accordance with the total number of its people; each person per year [shall be taxed] sixty-three cash in order to provide for the expense of making offerings [to the Emperor]."
又曰：“盖闻王者莫高于周文，伯者莫高于齐桓，皆待贤人而成名。 诏书又说：“常闻王者没有超过周文王的，霸者没有超过齐桓公的，都是依靠贤人辅佐而成名的。 [The edict] also said, "Verily [We] have heard that no [true] king was greater than [King] Wen of the Chou [dynasty] and no Lord Protector was greater than [Duke] Huan of Ch'i—both needed capable men in order to make a name for themselves.
今天下贤者智能，岂特古之人乎？ 当今天下的贤才智能之人难道不及古代的多吗？ At present in the world there are capable men who are wise and able; why should only men of ancient times [be capable]?
患在人主不交故也，士奚由进！ 只怕人主不尊重人才，人才就无从发现与发挥作用！ The trouble is that the ruler of men does not meet them. By what means could a gentleman have access [to me]?
今吾以天之灵、贤士大夫定有天下，以为一家，欲其长久，世世奉宗庙亡绝也。 今日我仰仗上天的威灵依靠贤士大夫的支持而取得天下，成为刘汉一统，想长久传业，代代奉宗庙而不要亡绝。 Now I, by the spiritual power of Heaven, [and by my] capable gentlemen and high officials have subjugated and possess the empire and have made it one family. I wish it to be enduring, that generation after generation should worship at my ancestral temple without cessation.
贤人已与我共平之矣，而不与吾共安利之，可乎？ 各位贤才曾帮助我共定天下，却不能和我共享荣华，行吗？ Capable persons have already shared with me in its pacification. Should it be that [any capable persons] are not to share together with me in its comfort and its benefits?
贤士大夫有肯从我游者，吾能尊显之。 贤士大夫有肯跟我共事的，我一定能让他们显贵。 If there are any capable gentlemen or sirs who are willing to follow and be friends with me, I can make them honorable and illustrious.
布告天下，使明知朕意。 把这个意思布告天下，使大家都明白朕的心意。 Let [the foregoing] be published to [all] the world, to make plain Our intention.
御史大夫昌下相国，相国酂侯下诸侯王，御史中执法下郡守，其有意称明德者，必身劝，为之驾，遣诣相国府，署行、义、年。 御史大夫周昌把诏书下达给相国，相国酂侯萧何下达给诸侯王，御史中执法下达给郡守，那些有美名和美德相称的人，一定要亲自去劝勉，为他们准备车马，送到相国府，登记他们的品行、仪表和年龄。 Let the Grandee Secretary [Chao] Yao transmit it to the Chancellor of State; let the Chancellor of State [Hsiao Ho], the Marquis of Tso, transmit it to the vassal kings; the Palace Secretary for Administrating the Laws shall transmit it to the Commandery Administrators. If any [among their people] have an excellent reputation and manifest virtue, [the officials] must personally urge [them to come], provide them with a quadriga, and send them to go to the courts of the Chancellor of State to have written down their accomplishments, their appearance, and their age.
有而弗言，觉，免。 有这样的人却不举荐，一旦发现，就免除官职。 If there are [such ones] and [any official] does not report them, when [this fact] becomes known, he shall be dismissed.
年老癃病，勿遣。” 年老体弱有病的，就不要送来。” Those who are aged, infirm, or ill should not be sent."

For all [the foregoing, the region] is in debt to his ability.

它稽首称臣。 赵佗叩首称臣。 [Chao] T'o made obeisance [to the Emperor's edict] and acknowledged himself as [Kao-tsu's] subject.
六月，令士卒从入蜀、汉、关中者皆复终身。 六月，诏令凡是士卒从高祖进入蜀、汉、关中的一律免除劳役终身。 In the sixth month [the Emperor] ordered that the officers and soldiers who had followed him into Shu, Hans, and Kuan-chung should all be exempted [from taxes and military service] for life.
秋七月，淮南王布反。 秋七月，淮南王英布反。 In the autumn, the seventh month, the King of Huai-nan, [Ch'ing] Pu, revolted.

上召见，薛公言布形势，上善之，封薛公千户。 皇上立即召见，薛公分析了英布的形势，皇上甚为满意，加封薛公千户。 and the Emperor saw his excellency. His excellency Hsieh told about [Ch'ing] Pu's circumstances, and the Emperor approved his [plan] and appointed his excellency Hsieh [to the income of] a thousand families.

人或闻，以语郦商。 有人闻皇上死讯，就告诉了郦商。 Someone heard of it and spoke to Li Shang.
郦商见审食其曰：“闻帝已崩四日，不发丧，欲诛诸将。 郦商见审食其说：“听说皇帝已去世，四日仍不发丧，想诛杀诸侯。 [Li] Shang saw Shen Yi-chi and said [to him], "I have heard that the Emperor has already been dead for four days, and that [the Empress] has not proclaimed any mourning and wishes to kill the generals.
诚如此，天下危矣。 果真如此，天下就危险了。 If [the situation] is really like this, the empire is certainly in danger.
陈平、灌婴将十万守荥阳，樊哙、周勃将二十万定燕、代，此闻帝崩，诸将皆诛，必连兵还乡，以攻关中。 陈平、灌婴领十万重兵守荥阳，樊哙、周勃领二十万重兵镇燕、代，他们要是听到皇上已崩，要诛诸将，将会联兵向京都进发，夺取关中。 Ch'en P'ing and Kuan Ying, leading a hundred thousand [men], defend Jung yang; Fan K'uai and Chou P'o, leading two hundred thousand [men], are subjugating Yen and Tai— when these [people] hear that the Emperor has died and the generals have all been killed, they will certainly turn about face with their troops in order to attack Kuan-chung.
大臣内畔，诸将外反，亡可跷足待也。” 大臣内叛，诸将外反，败亡就是旦夕间事了。” With the great ministers revolting inside [the passes] and the generals turning against [the dynasty] outside [the passes], it could await its destruction on tiptoe."
审食其入言之，乃以丁未发丧，大赦天下。 审食其将此言转呈吕后，吕后就在四月二十八日发丧，宣布大赦天下。 Shen Yi-chi entered and told these [words to the Empress]. So on [the day] ting-wei [the Empress] proclaimed a mourning and granted a general amnesty to the world.
五月丙寅，葬长陵。 五月十七日，葬皇帝于长陵。 In the fifth month, on [the day] ping-yin, [the Emperor] was buried in the Ch'ang Tomb.
已下，皇太子、群臣皆反至太上皇庙。 已下棺，皇太子群臣都回到太上皇庙。 When [the coffin] had been put in place, the imperial heir-apparent and the courtiers all returned and went to the temple of the Grand Emperor.
群臣曰：“帝起细微，拨乱世反之正，平定天下，为汉太祖，功最高。” 君臣说：“皇帝崛起于平民，力挽狂澜还于正道，平定天下，成为汉朝开国祖先，功劳最高。” The courtiers said, "The Emperor arose from humble [beginnings]; he established order in a troubled generation, and turned it back to the right [path]. He pacified and subjugated the world, and became the Grand Founder of the Han [dynasty]. His achievements were very great.
上尊号曰高皇帝。 上尊号名为高皇帝。 We offer him the high title of Kao-huang-ti."
初，高祖不修文学，而性明达，好谋，能听，自监门戍卒，见之如旧。 原先，高祖不大注意书本知识，而秉性开朗聪明，善于思考，能博采众议，从人民及官吏到普通士卒，都能平易相亲。 In his early life, Kao-tsu did not cultivate literary studies, but by nature he was intelligent and penetrating. He liked to make plans and was able to listen [to others]. From a superintendent of a gate or a man exiled to the frontier [upwards, anyone] who came to see him [was treated] as an old [friend].
初顺民心作三章之约。 入关之初顺民心作约法三章。 At the beginning [of his reign] he conformed to the people's wishes when he made an agreement [with them] in three articles.
天下既定，命萧何次律令，韩信申军法，张苍定章程，叔孙通制礼仪，陆贾造《新语》。 天下平定之后，命萧何整理法律与条令，命韩信建立与健全军队法纪，命张苍统一历法与度量衡等法式，令叔孙通制定各种礼节与仪式，令陆贾作总结历史兴亡之鉴的《新语》。 when the empire had been subjugated, he commanded Hsiao Ho to set in order the [criminal] laws and orders, Han Hsin to set forth the military methods, Chang Ts'ang to fix the calendar and measures, Shu-sun T'ung to establish the rites and etiquette, and Lu Chia to compose the Hsin-yü.
又与功臣剖符作誓，丹书铁契，金匮石室，藏之宗庙。 又与各功臣剖符作誓，丹书铁契，贮于金柜石室之中，保藏于宗庙之内。 With his meritorious followers he split tallies and made oaths, with red writing and an iron certificate, a golden box and a stone chest, and kept them in the ancestral temple.
虽日不暇给，规摹弘远矣。 高祖虽然日理万机，但订立的规划与矩度是意义深远的。 Altho daily no leisure was afforded him, his designs and plans were vast and far-reaching.
赞曰：《春秋》晋史蔡墨有言：陶唐氏既衰，其后有刘累，学扰龙，事孔甲，范氏其后也。 班固评论：《春秋》晋国史官蔡墨说过：唐尧所建的陶唐氏衰微了，其后有刘累，学驯龙之术，臣事于夏天子孔甲，晋国范氏是他的后代。 In eulogy we say, In `Spring and Autumn' [times], the historian of [the state of] Chin, Ts'ai Mo, said, "When the T'ao and T'ang family had lost its power, among its descendants there was a Liu Lei who learned to train dragons. He served K'ung-chia. The Fan family were his descendants."
而大夫范宣子亦曰：“祖自虞以上为陶唐氏，在夏为御龙氏，在商为豕韦氏，在周为唐杜氏，晋主夏盟为范氏。” 而晋大夫范宣子亦说过：“我的祖先从虞以上为陶唐民，在夏代为御龙氏，在商代为豕韦氏，在周为唐杜氏，在晋为霸主时为范氏。” Moreover the Grandee Fan Hsientzu also said, "My ancestors before [the time of] Yü [Shun] were surnamed T'ao and T'ang; in the [time of the] Hsia [dynasty], they were surnamed Yü-lung; in [the time of] the Shang [dynasty], they were surnamed Shih-wei; in [the time of] the Chou [dynasty], they were surnamed T'ang and Tu; when [the state of] Chin became the lord of China's oaths, they were surnamed Fan.
范氏为晋士师，鲁文公世奔秦。 范氏为晋正卿，鲁文公时出奔秦国。 A [member of the] Fan [family] was the Supreme Judge of Chin. In the time of Duke Wen of Lu, [the family] fled to Ch'in.
后归于晋，其处者为刘氏。 后归于晋，其留居秦地的为刘氏。 Later they returned to Chin. Those [of the Fan family] who remained [in Ch'in] became the Liu family."
刘向云战国时刘氏自秦获于魏。 刘向说成国时刘氏从秦复居于魏。 Liu Hsiang said, "In the time of the Contending States, [a member of] the Liu family from Ch'in was made prisoner of war by Weih.
秦灭魏，迁大梁，都于丰，故周市说雍齿曰：“丰，故梁徙也。” 秦攻魏，魏都迁徙于大梁，都于丰地，所以周市劝说雍齿说：“丰，是魏的迁徙之处。” When [the state of] Ch'in destroyed [the state of] Weih, [the family] moved to Ta-liang and dwelt at Feng. Hence Chou Fu said to Yung Ch'ih, `Feng was formerly a colony of Liang.'
是以颂高祖云：“汉帝本系，出自唐帝。降及于周，在秦作刘。涉魏而东，遂为丰公。” 据此以赞颂汉高祖说：“汉帝的本系，出自于陶唐尧帝。传世到周，在秦姓刘。经魏而向东，于是为丰公。” Thus the eulogy of Kao-tsu said, `The line of descent of the Han Emperor Is traced from the Emperor T'ang [Yao]. Coming on down to the Chou [dynasty], In [the state of] Ch'in it became the Liu [family]. It crossed into Weih and went eastwards. Thereupon [its head] became the Lord of Feng.' "
丰公，盖太上皇父。 丰公，可能是太上皇之父。 The Lord of Feng was indeed the Grand Emperor's father.
其迁日浅，坟墓在丰鲜焉。 其迁丰之日不久，坟墓在丰地的不多。 The period since his moving [to Feng] had been brief, [for] there are few mounds or graves [of the family] at Feng.
及高祖即位，置祠祀官，则有秦、晋、梁、荆之巫，世祠天地，缀之以祀，岂不信哉！ 到高祖即位，设置祠祀之官，于是有秦、晋、梁、荆的祖庙，世世祠祭天地祖先香火连绵，这是有根源的啊！ When Kao-tsu took the throne, he established officials for the worship [of his ancestors], so there were shamans from Ch'in, Chin, Liang, and Ching. For generations the worship of Heaven and Earth has been accompanied by the worship [of these ancestors]. How could [these facts] be untrustworthy?
由是推之，汉承尧运，德祚已盛，断蛇著符，旗帜上赤，协于火德，自然之应，得天统矣。 以此推断，汉承尧运，德行气数正逢盛时，断蛇而合“白帝子为赤帝子所杀”的谶言，旗帜以红色为主，这是火德的象征，以火代木的自然感应，正符合上天统序的规律。 From the foregoing [accounts] we infer that the Han [dynasty] succeeded to the fortunes of Yao; its virtues and the happiness recompensing it are already great. The cutting in two of the snake, the auspicious omens which appeared, the banners and pennons which emphasized [the color] red in harmony with the virtue of fire, were responses which came of their own accord, [thereby showing that Kao-tsu] secured the [dynastic] rule from Heaven.
