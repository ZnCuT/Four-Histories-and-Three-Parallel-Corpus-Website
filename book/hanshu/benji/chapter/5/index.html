孝成皇帝，元帝太子也。母曰王皇后。 孝成皇帝，是汉元帝的太子。母亲是王皇后。 Emperor Hsiao-ch'eng was the Heir-apparent of Emperor Yüan. His mother was entitled the Empress [née] Wang.
元帝在太子宫生甲观画堂，为世嫡皇孙。 元帝在太子宫时，（成帝）出生在甲观的画堂，成为世系中的嫡皇孙。 While Emperor Yüan [was living] in the Heir-apparent's Palace, [the future Emperor Ch'eng] was born in the Painted Hall of the First Lodge. He was [called] the Imperial Grandson Who is the Heir by the First Wife.
宣帝爱之，字曰太孙，常置左右。 汉宣帝喜爱他，给他取字为太孙，常常把他放在自己身边。 Emperor Hsüan loved him, named him the Heir-apparent of the Heir-apparent, and constantly kept him about himself.
年三岁而宣帝崩，元帝即位，帝为太子。 三岁时宣帝去世，元帝即位，成帝成为太子。 When he was in his third year, Emperor Hsüan died, and Emperor Yüan ascended the throne, [whereupon the future] Emperor became the Heir-apparent.
壮好经书，宽博谨慎。 成年后喜好儒家经典，心胸宽广，为人宽厚，谨慎细心。 When he grew up, he loved the classics, and was large-minded and generous, circumspect and attentive.
初居桂宫，上尝急召，太子出龙楼门，不敢绝驰道，西至直城门，得绝乃度，还入作室门。 起初居住在桂宫，元帝曾经紧急召见他，太子走出龙楼门，不敢横穿皇帝专用的驰道，向西走到直城门，得到允许后才横穿，然后返回进入作室门。 Previously, when the Heir-apparent lived in Kuei Palace, Emperor [Yüan] once summoned him [to come] in haste. He went out of the Lung-lou Gate [to his palace], but did not dare to cross the imperial pathway, so went west to the Chih-ch'eng Gate [of the city], where it was permitted to cross [the imperial pathway]. Thereupon he crossed, returned [eastwards], and entered the Artisan's Chamber Gate [of Wei-yang Palace].
上迟之，问其故，以状对。 元帝嫌他来晚了，询问原因，太子把情况如实回答。 The Emperor [said] he was tardy, and asked the reason for it. [The Heir-apparent] replied, [giving] the circumstances.
上大说，乃著令，令太子得绝驰道云。 元帝非常高兴，于是发布命令，允许太子横穿驰道。 and the Emperor was greatly pleased. Thereupon [the Emperor] published an ordinance, ordering that an Heir-apparent should be allowed to cross the imperial pathway.
其后幸酒，乐燕乐，上不以为能。 后来（太子）喜好饮酒，热衷于宴饮作乐，元帝认为他没有才能。 Later, [the Heir-apparent grew] fond of wine and "took pleasure in the delights of conviviality," [so that] Emperor Yüan did not think him capable.
而定陶恭王有材艺，母傅昭仪又爱幸，上以故常有意欲以恭王为嗣。 而定陶恭王有才能技艺，他的母亲傅昭仪又受到宠爱，元帝因此常常有立恭王为继承人的想法。 Moreover [Emperor Yüan's second son], King Kung of Ting-t'ao, [Liu K'ang], showed innate ability and talents; his mother, the Brilliant Companion [née] Fu, was also loved and favored [by the Emperor]; for this reason the Emperor constantly had thoughts of wanting to make King Kung his heir.
赖侍中史丹护太子家，辅助有力，上亦以先帝尤爱太子，故得无废。 依靠侍中史丹保护太子一家，辅助有力，元帝也因为先帝特别喜爱太子，所以太子才没有被废黜。 [But] because of the Palace Attendant Shih Tan, who protected the Heir-apparent's household and effectively aided and assisted [the Heir-apparent], and because, moreover, the deceased Emperor [Hsüan] had especially loved the Heir-apparent, Emperor [Yüan] permitted him not to be dismissed.

The highest ministers should be very diligent, should lead and act as examples to the many officials, and should support Us in Our inadequacies.

昔成汤受命，列为三代，而祭祀废绝。 从前成汤接受天命，（商朝）被列为三代之一，但祭祀已经废止断绝。 Anciently, T'ang the Victorious received [Heaven's] mandate and [his dynasty, the Yin,] is grouped among the Three Dynasties, yet his sacrifices have been neglected and cut off.
考求其后，奠正孔吉。其封吉为殷绍嘉侯。” 考察寻求他的后代，孔吉是合适的人选。封孔吉为殷绍嘉侯。” When [We] sought for his descendants, none was more upright than K'ung Chi. Let [K'ung] Chi be enfeoffed as the Marquis Continuing and Honoring the Yin [Dynasty]."
三月，进爵为公，及周承休侯皆为公，地各百里。 三月，晋升爵位为公，和周承休侯都成为公，封地各一百里。 In the third month, his noble rank was advanced to be that of Duke. Together with the Marquis who Succeeds to the Greatness of the Chou [Dynasty, Chi Tang], they were both made Dukes, each with a territory of a hundred li [square].
行幸雍，祠五畤。 成帝驾临雍地，祭祀五畤。 [The Emperor] traveled and favored Yung [with a visit, where he] sacrificed at the altars to the Five [Lords on High].
夏四月，以大司马票骑将军为大司马，罢将军官。 夏季四月，将大司马骠骑将军改为大司马，撤销将军官职。 In the summer, the fourth month, the Commander-in-chief and General of Agile Cavalry, [Wang] Ken, was made [merely] Commander-in-chief, and his General's office was abolished.
御史大夫为大司空，封为列侯。益大司马、大司空奉如丞相。 御史大夫改为大司空，封为列侯。增加大司马、大司空的俸禄如同丞相。 [The title of] the Grandee Secretary was made that of the Grand Minister of Works, and [its incumbent, Ho Wu,] was enfeoffed as a full marquis. The salaries of the Commander-in-chief and the Grand Minister of Works were increased to be like that of the Lieutenant Chancellor.
秋八月庚戌，中山王兴薨。 秋季八月庚戌日，中山王刘兴去世。 In the autumn, the eighth month, on [the day] keng-hsü, the King of Chung-shan, [Liu] Hsing, died.
冬十一月，立楚孝王孙景为定陶王。 冬季十一月，立楚孝王的孙子刘景为定陶王。 In the winter, the eleventh month, [the Emperor] set up [Liu] Ching, a grandson of King Hsiao of Ch'u, [Liu Ao], as King of Ting-t'ao.
定陵侯淳于长大逆不道，下狱死。 定陵侯淳于长犯大逆不道之罪，被关进监狱死去。 The Marquis of Ting-ling, Shun-yü Chang, [who had committed] treason and inhuman conduct, was sent to prison and died.
廷尉孔光使持节赐贵人许氏药，饮药死。 廷尉孔光持符节赐贵人许氏毒药，许氏饮药而死。 and the Commandant of Justice, K'ung Kuang, was sent with credentials to grant poison to the honored lady née Hsü. She drank the poison and died.
十二月，罢部刺史，更置州牧，秩二千石。 十二月，撤销部刺史，改设州牧，俸禄为二千石。 In the twelfth month, the Inspectors of Regional Divisions were abolished and there were established instead Provincial Shepherds with the rank of two thousand piculs.
二年春正月，行幸甘泉，郊泰畤。 绥和二年春正月，成帝驾临甘泉宫，在泰畤举行郊祀。 In the second year, in the spring, the first month, [the Emperor] traveled and favored Kan-ch'üan [Palace with a visit, where he performed] the suburban sacrifice at the altar to the Supreme [One].
二月壬子，丞相翟方进薨。 二月壬子日，丞相翟方进去世。 In the second month, on [the day] jen-tzu, the Lieutenant Chancellor, Chai Fang-chin, died.
三月，行幸河东，祠后土。 三月，成帝驾临河东郡，祭祀后土。 In the third month, [the Emperor] traveled and favored Ho-tung [Commandery with a visit, where he] sacrificed to Sovereign Earth.
丙戌，帝崩于未央宫。 丙戌日，成帝在未央宫去世。 On [the day] ping-hsü, the Emperor died in Wei-yang Palace.
皇太后诏有司复长安南北郊。 皇太后诏令有关部门恢复长安南北郊的祭祀。 The Empress Dowager [née Wang] issued an imperial edict that the high officials should reestablish the [places] for the suburban sacrifices to the south and north of Ch'ang-an.
四月己卯，葬延陵。 四月己卯日，安葬在延陵。 In the fourth month, on [the day] yi-mao, [the Emperor] was buried in the Yen Tomb.
赞曰：臣之姑充后宫为婕妤，父子昆弟侍帷幄，数为臣言： 赞曰：我的姑姑进入后宫成为婕妤，父亲、儿子、兄弟在宫中侍奉，多次对我说： In eulogy we say: Your servant [Pan Piao's] paternal aunt was given a place in the [imperial] harem and became a Favorite Beauty; her father, [Pan K'uang], his sons and her brothers waited [upon the Emperor] in his private apartments, and frequently said to your servant,
成帝善修容仪，升车正立，不内顾，不疾言，不亲指，临朝渊嘿，尊严若神，可谓穆穆天子之容者矣！ 成帝善于修饰仪容举止，登上车驾时端正站立，不回头看，不急促说话，不亲自用手指点，临朝时深沉静默，尊严如同神明，可以说是有天子端庄的仪容啊！ "Emperor Ch'eng was good at cultivating his deportment and appearance. `When he mounted his chariot, he stood upright, he did not look around, he did not speak hastily, he did not point with his hands.' When he attended court, he was profound and silent, dignified and grave like a god, so that it might be [truly] said, Majestic is the bearing of the Son of Heaven.
博览古今，容受直辞。公卿称职，奏议可述。 博览古今典籍，能够容纳接受直言。公卿称职，奏议值得记述。 He read widely on [both] ancient and present [matters], and received frank discourses indulgently." His ministers were worthy of their positions, and their memorials and discussions are worthy of being transmitted [to posterity].
遭世承平，上下和睦。然湛于酒色，赵氏乱内，外家擅朝，言之可为於邑。 遭遇承平时代，上下和睦。然而沉湎于酒色，赵氏在宫内作乱，外戚专擅朝政，说起这些真令人叹息。 He happened upon an age which inherited a peaceful condition, when the superior and his inferiors were in concord. Yet he gave himself up to wine and women. The Chao clan caused disorder within [the palace] and his maternal relatives made themselves masters in the court. In saying this, I cannot but feel oppressed.
建始以来，王氏始执国命，哀、平短祚，莽遂篡位，盖其威福所由来者渐矣！ 建始年间以来，王氏开始执掌国家命运，哀帝、平帝在位时间短暂，王莽于是篡夺皇位，大概他的权势和福分是逐渐形成的啊！ From [the period] Chien-shih onwards, the Wang clan first grasped the power of the state. [Emperors] Ai and P'ing had [only] short lives, and [Wang] Mang thereupon usurped the throne. In fact his [usurpation of the imperial power, thereby enabling him to grant] severity or favor, came about [very] gradually.
