令诸侯无人贡，弛山泽，减诸服御，损郎吏员，发仓庚以振民，民得卖爵。 诏令诸侯不得向朝廷纳贡，解除对山泽开采的禁令，减省多项公务开支，精简冗员，打开各地粮仓赈济灾民，有爵级的人可以卖爵。 [The Emperor] ordered that the nobles should not pay tribute. He opened [to the common people] the mountains and marshes, reduced the [imperial] robes and the imperial officers, diminished the [regular] number of Gentlemen and officials, and opened the granaries in order to succor the people. The people were allowed to sell [aristocratic] ranks.
七年夏，六月己亥，帝崩于未央宫。 七年夏六月初一日，文帝驾崩于未央宫。 In the seventh year, in the summer, the sixth month, on [the day] chi-hai, the Emperor died in the Wei-yang Palace.
遗诏曰：“朕闻之：盖天下万物之萌生，靡不有死。 留下遗诏说：“朕听说：大凡天下万物从萌生以后，都要走向死亡。 His testamentary decree said, "We have heard it [said that], of all [plants and animals] that sprout from or are born to any of the beings in the world, all of them, it seems, have to die.
死者天地之理，物之自然，奚可甚哀！ 死亡是天地间的常理，事物的自然规律，何必为此而恸哀！ Death is a law of Heaven and Earth, and the nature of things. [Then] how could it be [so] greatly lamentable?
当今之世，咸嘉生而恶死，厚葬以破业，重服以伤生，吾甚不取。 现今之世，都爱生而恶死，厚葬以致浪费财业，重服以致伤害民生，我极不赞同。 "当今之世，咸嘉生而恶死，厚葬以破业，重服以伤生，吾甚不取。
且朕既不德，无以佐百姓。 何况朕德行浅薄，未能给百姓带来福祉。 Moreover, since I am not virtuous, I have been unable to assist the people.
今崩，又使重服久临，以罹寒暑之数，哀人父子； 在去世之后，又进行长时间的隆重的吊唁活动，使临丧者遭受在寒暑中受尽丧礼的折磨，让别人的子弟为我尽哀。 Now that I die, if I cause people to wear heavy mourning and mourn for a long time, suffering from the extremes of cold and heat, grieving for their parents and children.
伤长老之志，损其饮食，绝鬼神之祭祀，以重吾不德，谓天下何！ 就会令别人的长老感到伤神；损坏其他鬼神的祭馔来丰盛对我的祭馔，就会使其他的鬼神因之饥饿，这就更加增重了我的罪过，这叫我如何向天下交代！ hurting the feelings of the elders, reducing their food and drink, and interrupting the sacrifices to the spirits and divinities, thereby increasing my lack of virtue—what can I say to the world?
朕获保宗庙，以眇眇之身托于天下君王之上，二十有余年矣。 朕继承大统，以细末之身托于天下君王之上，已经二十三年了。 I have had the good fortune to protect the ancestral temples, and with my humble person have been entrusted with a position above the kings of the world for more than twenty years.
赖天之灵。 依赖于上天的威灵。 By the grace of Heaven.
社稷之福，方内安宁，靡有兵革。 社稷的洪福，四海安宁，未有战乱。 the blessing of the state, the interior is peaceful, and there has been no warfare.
朕既不敏，常畏过行，以羞先帝之遗德； 朕秉性愚钝，常恐言行有过失，以玷辱先帝的煌煌帝德。 I am not intelligent, and have always feared improper conduct that would disgrace the legacy of virtue left by the former emperors.
惟年之久长，惧于不终。 而在位时间愈久，更恐不能善始善终。 As the years have passed, I have feared not being able to end well.
今乃幸以天年得复供养于高庙，朕之不明与嘉之，其奚哀念之有！ 今日幸以自然的死亡得以供养于刘汉的祖庙之中，这实在是对我这个薄德之人最大的嘉勉，这还有什么应该哀恸的！ Now I am fortunate to have lived out my natural years and to be able to return to and be供养 in the Temple of Kao-tsu. Though I am not wise, I rejoice in this—what is there to grieve about!
其令天下吏民，令到出临三日，皆释服。 可令天下吏民，在接到志哀令后三天即停止悼念活动。 "Order all officials and people in the empire: upon receiving this order, they shall mourn for three days, then all remove their mourning garments.
无禁取妇、嫁女、祠祀、饮酒、食肉。 不得禁止在国丧期间举行嫁娶、祭祀的饮酒食肉。 There shall be no prohibition on taking a wife, marrying off a daughter, offering sacrifices, drinking wine, or eating meat.
自当给丧事服临者，皆无践。 凡必须参加葬礼的，都不要大量披麻戴孝。 Those who must attend the funeral and wear mourning shall not wear unhemmed mourning clothes.
绖带无过三寸。 其头与腰的孝带宽不过三寸。 The hemp headbands and girdles shall not be more than three inches wide.
无布车及兵器。 不要用布帛来铺盖灵车及派羽林军护灵。 There shall be no display of carriages or weapons.
无发民哭临宫殿中。 不要派百姓到宫殿中来哭灵。 Do not send people to wail in the palaces.
殿中当临者，皆以旦夕各十五举音，礼皆罢。 朝廷中应参加葬礼的，只能在早晚与大功时可以有哭泣的哀音。礼毕就停止。 Those in the palace who must mourn shall wail fifteen times each morning and evening; after the rites, this shall cease.
非旦夕临时，禁无得擅哭临。 不是早晚与举行葬礼时，禁止擅自放声哭泣。 Except during the morning and evening mourning times, no one shall be allowed to wail without permission.
以下，服大红十五日，小红十四日，纤七日，释服。 下棺后，服大功(丧服)十五日，小功十四日，细布衣七日，三十六天以后全部释去孝服。 After the interment, wear heavy mourning for fifteen days, light mourning for fourteen days, and plain clothes for seven days; then remove all mourning garments.
它不在令中者，皆以此令比类从事。 其他有关事项在令中没提及的，都以此令类推。 Whatever is not mentioned in this order shall be handled in accordance with the spirit of this order.
布告天下，使明知朕意。 布告天下，使明知朕意。 Proclaim this to the world so that all clearly understand My will.
霸陵山川因其故，无有所改。 霸陵川山按其原貌，不要另造坟陵。 The mountains and rivers around the Pa Tomb shall remain as they are; no changes shall be made.
归夫人以下至少使。” 宫中夫人以下的都遣送归家。” Return all ladies below the rank of夫人 down to the Junior Maids to their homes."
令中尉亚夫为车骑将军，属国悍为将屯将军，郎中令张武为复士将军，发近县卒万六千人，发内史卒万五千人，臧郭、穿、复土属将军武。 令中尉周亚夫为车骑将军，属国悍为将屯将军，郎中令张武为陵墓复土将军。征发近县士卒一万六千人，内史卒一万五千人，交给张武将军从事开挖墓井与复土回填工作。 Order the Palace Military Commander [Chou] Ya-fu to be General of Chariots and Cavalry, the [Director of] Dependent States [Hsü] Tao to be General In Charge of Encampments, and the Chief of the Gentlemen-at-the-Palace Chang Wu to be the General [In Charge of] Replacing the Earth. Mobilize 16,000 soldiers from neighboring prefectures and 15,000 soldiers from the inner history's office; the tasks of burying the outer coffin, digging, and refilling the earth shall be under the charge of General [Chang] Wu.
赐诸侯王以下至孝悌、力田金、钱、帛各有数。 赐各王侯以下至孝悌力田的人金钱帛各若干。 Grant gold, cash, and silk to vassal kings and those below them down to the Filially Pious, Fraternally Respectful, and [Diligent] Cultivators of the Soil, each in a specified amount.
乙巳，葬霸陵。 六月初七日，葬于霸陵。 On [the day] yi-szu, [the Emperor] was buried in the Pa Tomb.
赞曰：孝文皇帝即位二十三年，宫室、苑囿、车骑、服御无所增益。 班固评论：孝文皇帝在位二十三年，宫殿御苑车骑服御无所增加。 In eulogy we say: Emperor Hsiao-wen occupied the throne for twenty-three years. In his palaces and apartments, in his pastures and enclosures, in his carriages and saddle-horses, in his garments and daily needs, he made no addition or increase [over the requirements of his predecessors].
有不便，辄弛以利民。 当百姓感到不便时，常放开禁令以利百姓。 If there was [any activity] that was not convenient [for the people], he at once abandoned it in order to benefit his people.
尝欲作露台，召匠计之，直百金。 他曾想造一座报时辰的露台，召工匠预算，造价需百金。 At one time he wanted to make a roofless terrace. He summoned the artisans to count up its [cost], and it would cost [the equivalent of] a hundred [catties of] gold.
上曰：“百金，中人十家之产也。 文帝说：“百金是中等人家十户的产业。 The Emperor said, "A hundred [catties of] gold is the estate of ten families of medium [means].
吾奉先帝宫室，常恐羞之，何以台为！” 我继守先帝的宫室，常恐有所玷污，何必要筑露台！” I have received the palaces and apartments of the deceased emperors and have constantly been afraid that I should disgrace them; what is the use of building this terrace?"
身衣弋绨，所幸慎夫人衣不曳地，帷帐无文绣，以示敦朴，为天下先。 身穿黑色绨衣，所宠爱的慎夫人衣长不拖地，帷帐不绣花，以表示自己的敦朴，为天下的表率。 He personally dressed in thick black silk. The clothes of his favorite, the Lady [née] Shen, did not trail on the ground. His curtains and canopies had no ornaments or embroidery, thereby showing that in naturalness and simplicity he was the leader of the world.
治霸陵，皆瓦器，不得以金、银、铜、锡为饰，因其山，不起坟。 修造霸陵陵墓，都用瓦器，不准以金银铜锡装饰，因山起陵，不另选坟。 When he constructed the Pa Tomb, he altogether [used] objects of pottery and did not allow the use of gold, silver, copper, or tin as ornaments. He took advantage of [the rise of] the hill [where] his [grave was built], and did not raise a mound [upon his tomb].
南越尉佗自立为帝，召贵佗兄弟，以德怀之，佗遂称臣。 南越尉佗自立为帝，文帝贵封尉佗兄弟，以德义进行感召，佗又重新称臣。 When the Commandant of Nan-yüeh, [Chao] T'o, set himself up as Emperor, [Emperor Wen] summoned and honored [Chao] T'o's older and younger cousins and enveloped them with his goodness; thereupon [Chao] T'o declared himself his subject.
与匈奴结和亲，后而背约入盗，令边备守，不发兵深入，恐烦百姓。 与匈奴结为友好，不久匈奴背约入盗，文帝只令边兵加强防守，不发兵反攻其地，恐增加百姓负担。 After he had made peace and friendship with the Huns, and they had gone contrary to their covenant and entered [his territory] to rob, he ordered that the borders should prepare for defence, but did not send troops deep into [the Hun territory], for fear of burdening his subjects.
吴王诈病不朝，赐以几杖。 吴王诈称病不朝，文帝赏以几杖进行慰问。 When the King of Wu, [Liu P'i], feigned illness and did not come to court, [the Emperor] granted him a stool and a cane.
群臣袁盎等谏说虽切，常假借纳用焉。 群臣袁盎等虽极力谏说不应为吴王所欺，文帝仍将几杖送给吴王使用。 When the officials, Yüan Ang and others, remonstrated [with him], although their words were cutting, he often pardoned them, accepted [their advice], and put it into practise.
张武等受赂金钱，觉，更加赏赐，以愧其心。 张武等受贿金钱，发觉后，更加赏赐，使其惭愧自省。 When Chang Wu and others accepted bribes of gold or cash, and [the fact] was discovered, [the Emperor] gave to them still more presents and grants, in order to shame them.
专务以德化民，是以海内殷富，兴于礼义，断狱数百，几致刑措。 文帝专务以德化民，是以四海殷富，大兴礼义之风，断狱仅数百人，几乎到刑罚不用。 His sole care was to improve the people by means of his virtue; for this reason [all the country] within the [four] seas was prosperous and opulent and advanced towards proper conduct and right relationships. [During his reign] there were pronounced verdicts [of capital punishment] in [only] several hundred [cases]; [so that he] almost succeeded in setting aside punishments [without using them].
呜呼，仁哉！ 唉，仁德之帝啊！ Alas! How benevolent he was!
